Frankie Knuckles

January 18, 1955 (died at 59 y/o)

Meet Frankie Knuckles, the legendary producer, remixer, and DJ who's often named the Godfather of house music. His innovative beats and infectious tunes revolutionized the scene, making a lasting impact during his heyday at Chicago's Warehouse nightclub from 1977 to 1987.

Lil Louis

May 31, 1962 (61 years old)

Many call him the Founding Father of House Music, we call him an absolute legend! Lil Louis is one of the Chicago House DJs scoring an international hit with "French Kiss". This Chicago-born legend owned a club called "the Future" from the late 70s, which later threw the biggest parties in town.

Fred again..

July 19,1993 (30 years old)

Fred again.. has been one of the most exciting artists to watch over the past few years. Selling out the Ziggo Dome in our own Amsterdam, causing earthquakes from all the people dancing at Down The Rabbit Hole in Belgium, and giving one of the most iconic Boiler Room session in London, 2023 really was a good year for him.
